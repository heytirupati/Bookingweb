Spiritual Journey

Sacred destinations that bring peace, devotion and divine connection

Tirupati – Tirumala

Home to the sacred Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage destinations in the world. Devotees believe Lord Venkateswara grants blessings for prosperity, health and spiritual growth.

Tiruvannamalai

A sacred destination dedicated to Lord Shiva as Arunachaleswara. Known for the holy Girivalam path around the Arunachala hill, it draws thousands of devotees seeking inner peace and enlightenment.

Padmavathi Temple

Located in Tiruchanur near Tirupati, the Padmavathi Ammavari temple is one of the most revered goddess temples in South India — an essential stop on every Tirupati darshan circuit.

Kapila Theertham

The only Shiva temple at the foothills of Tirumala, featuring a natural waterfall and sacred pond — a truly divine and serene experience for every pilgrim visiting Tirupati.
